Last Minute Bookings are offered by the circuits at a very special price due to short-notice availability. The prices and packages are only designed for circuit-booking.com and are normally much cheaper than the list prices. You can book such an offer right through our website and please feel free to add any comments to your booking in case you are missing some services in the offered service package.
